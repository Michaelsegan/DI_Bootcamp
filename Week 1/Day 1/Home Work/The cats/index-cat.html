About domestic cats

They are often called house cats when keptas indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them form other felines. they are often valued by humans for companionship.

There are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries..

Cat senses

  • They can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequencey for human ears
  • They ca see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and better sense of smell than humans
